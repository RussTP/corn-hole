RN HOLE

Chuck some Bean bags!

Gameplay and Scoring

Rules:The first team to reach or exceed 21 points wins, and the game is played until the end of an inning, even if a team goes over 21

Teams:Two teams of two players compete against each other.

Bags:Each team has four bags, and players alternate throwing their bags in each round.

Scoring:Bags that go through the hole score 3 points. Bags that land on the board and remain there score 1 point. Bags that hit the ground before or after landing on the board score 0 points
